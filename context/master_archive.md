# Black Belt Preparation Archive
## Goal
Primary objective: Successfully complete Black Belt grading on King's Birthday weekend 2027.

Grading demands:
- Saturday: 4 hours training (morning with 10kg vest), lunch run, full technical assessment.
- Sunday: 4 hours training, run, ~1 hour sparring (~26 bouts).

Training philosophy:
All conditioning, nutrition and recovery decisions are evaluated against their contribution to grading success.

# Key Conclusions
- Running is a support modality for karate.
- Primary requirement is technical precision under fatigue.
- Aerobic development has improved markedly.
- Recovery capacity is strong when nutrition is adequate.
- Nutrition consistency remains the main weakness.

# Recovery Trends
Historical RHR (2023-Feb 2026): 61-63 bpm typical.
Current baseline: 52-58 bpm.
Interpretation:
- 52-55 = fresh
- 56-59 = normal load
- 60+ = fatigue, poor recovery, illness or under-fuelling.
